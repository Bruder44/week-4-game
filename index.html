Mike Tyson's Random Number Knockout Game

How to Play


    You will have to help Mike Tyson add up to the random number given to you at the start of the game.

    There are four different colored punching bags below: Blue, Green, Red and Yellow.

    By clicking on a punching bag, you will add a specific amount of points to the total number you and Mike need to reach.

    In order to win, you must help Mike match the total score to the random number by clicking on the different punching bags.

    If you fail to help Mike, then you lose the game, this occurs when your total score goes over the target random number.

    The amount of points each punching bag adds is hidden from you until you click upon one.

    Every time one game ends, a new game will start, where the values of each punching bag is changed as well as the random number you need to reach.
